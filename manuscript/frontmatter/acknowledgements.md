## Acknowledgements {.unnumbered}

SNode.C grew out of practical networking work, teaching, experiments, and repeated attempts to keep communication software understandable as it becomes larger.

I am grateful to the students, colleagues, users, and reviewers whose questions forced the design vocabulary to become sharper. Questions about why a boundary exists are often more valuable than requests for another shortcut; they expose whether an architecture can still explain itself.

This book also depends on the wider open-source ecosystem around C++, CMake, Linux, TLS, HTTP, WebSocket, MQTT, databases, documentation tools, and typesetting. SNode.C is one framework, but it exists inside that larger technical culture.
